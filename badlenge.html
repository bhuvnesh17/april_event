Badlenge Dharti Ki Tasveer

Here are some versions that we will use.


Badlenge dharti ki tasveer ko hum, badlenge us asmaan ko
Hum divya jyoti se roshan krenge, andhero bhare is jahan ko

1)
Fahrayenge ham dharam dhvaj apna Ghor uuche shikhro pe jaakar
Jinki bulandi ko parvat bhi dekhenge, Woh Apni Nazre Utha Kar
Taiyar hai ham dene ko ab to, Samay ke Har ik Imtihaan ko
Hum divya jyoti se roshan krenge, andhero bhare is jahan ko

2)
Dekha hai jo vishwa shanti ka sapna, wo sakar hoga yakin hai
Ishwar hai jab saath apne to bolo, kami aur kis baat ki hai
Hai kiski himmat jo rokega aakar, sachayi ke karwah ko
Hum divya jyoti se roshan krenge, andhero bhare is jahan ko

3)
Chalenge sabhi brahmgyani jo milkar aur thame baho mein bahein
Sab mushkile khud hi has kar dikhayngi hamko to manzil ki rahein
Seechenge apne lahu se sada ham jeet ke har ik nishan ko
Hum divya jyoti se roshan krenge, andhero bhare is jahan ko
